About Mpire

Mpire Official is a Filipino hip-hop collective under OBP (Original Batang Pinas), repping raw street culture, Southeast-Asian grit, and unapologetic lyricism. Founded in the Philippines, the group brings together artists like Ransacc, DA¥TONA, $aint Dej, Spooki Blu, Blvgatti, and Kaser1200 — each pushing their own flavour while riding under one empire.

Mpire blends Tagalog bars with hard-hitting trap production, creating a sound that represents the streets they came from—ranging from gritty anthems to high-energy tracks that define the new sound of Pinoy hip-hop.
